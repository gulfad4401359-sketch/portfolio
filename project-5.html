Project 05

SaaS Onboarding Flow

Product DesignVue.js A/B TestingAnalytics

Jun 2022 — Aug 2022

Project Overview

Redesigned the trial onboarding for a B2B project management SaaS with 14,000 trial signups per month, reducing time-to-first-value and increasing trial-to-paid conversion.

Challenge

74% of users abandoned during a mandatory 11-step setup phase before experiencing the product’s core value. Average time to first meaningful action: 23 minutes.

Solution

Applied a jobs-to-be-done framework. Rebuilt onboarding around a single activation moment — creating a first project — achievable in under 90 seconds.

Tech Stack

Key Outcomes

  • Trial-to-paid conversion rate increased by 52% — from 8.3% to 12.6%
  • Time to first action reduced from 23 minutes to under 90 seconds
  • Onboarding abandonment dropped from 74% to 18%

Project 06

Healthcare Patient Portal

AccessibilityAngular WCAG 2.1UX Research

Mar 2022 — Jul 2022

Project Overview

Designed and built an accessible patient portal for a regional hospital network serving 120,000 patients, meeting WCAG 2.1 AA standards and integrating with FHIR-compliant medical record systems.

Challenge

38% of patients were over 65. The legacy portal had a System Usability Scale score of 38/100 and phone call volumes to the appointments line remained high.

Solution

Ran usability studies with 18 participants including 6 with disabilities. Implemented high-contrast mode, scalable typography, keyboard navigation, and full screen reader compatibility. Reduced appointment booking steps from 8 to 3.

Tech Stack
AngularTypeScript FHIR APIaxe-core

Key Outcomes

  • System Usability Scale score improved from 38 to 82 — classified as excellent
  • Appointment phone call volume reduced by 44% within the first month
  • Zero WCAG 2.1 AA violations on final accessibility audit
